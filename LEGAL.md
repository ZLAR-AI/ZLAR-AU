ZLAR-AU is open-source software provided by ZLAR Inc. under the Apache License 2.0.

## What ZLAR-AU Is

ZLAR-AU is a reporting tool. It reads audit logs produced by ZLAR Gate and generates formatted reports. It is not an AI system. It does not make decisions, intercept traffic, or control agent behaviour.

## What Reports Are

Reports generated by ZLAR-AU are derived from data recorded by ZLAR Gate. They reflect the gate's observations during the period covered. They are not independently verified and do not constitute:

- Legal advice
- Compliance certification or assurance
- Audit opinions
- Regulatory determinations

## Compliance Framework Mappings

ZLAR-AU maps audit events to compliance frameworks including PCI-DSS, SOC 2, OSFI B-13, SOX, and the EU AI Act. These mappings are informational only. They are intended to assist in understanding which audit events may be relevant to specific frameworks. They do not constitute a determination that your deployment meets any regulatory requirement. Consult qualified legal, compliance, and audit professionals for formal compliance guidance.

## What ZLAR-AU Does Not Do

ZLAR-AU does not:
- Guarantee compliance with any regulatory framework
- Ensure regulatory adherence
- Prevent audit failures
- Eliminate compliance risk
- Replace professional audit guidance
